This is a story about a rescued puppy named Lilu.

Lilu was found in a rubbish tip in Singapore at 8 weeks old. She was malnourished, sick, and covered in fleas. Fortunately, she was rescued and adopted by a couple who nursed her back to health and gave her a new home in England. Lilu now lives happily with her fur parents and new friends in the English countryside. Lilu is now certified as therapy dog, you can find her volunteering at the local hospice every Tuesday afternoon.
